barely talk to my friends anymore

15 March 2024

there's this friend from university i used to talk to every day, we'd meet for coffee, play video games on weekends, watch movies, i don't remember the last time we talked, maybe october, maybe before that

it's not that anything happened, no fight, no falling out, just work took over everything and somehow the social calendar never got refilled after a certain point

the weird part is that i don't feel lonely in an acute way most of the time, i'm busy enough that it doesn't hit me until i'm doing something quiet and i realize i haven't actually talked to anyone i care about in weeks, and then it does hit and it feels heavier than i expected

the thing about friendships is that they need maintenance in a way that nobody really prepares you for, when you're in school it's automatic, you're in the same place every day, you don't have to try, when you're working full time in a demanding job you have to actively decide to make time and i keep letting that decision slip

i sent a few messages this week, made plans that actually happened, that felt good, it's a small thing but i think small things are how you fix this kind of drift
