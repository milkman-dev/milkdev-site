burnout

17 July 2024

i think i've been burned out for a while without knowing what to call it

it felt like tiredness at first, just needing more sleep than usual, then it started feeling like lack of motivation, like maybe i just wasn't cut out for this kind of work, i started wondering if the problem was me, if i was fundamentally bad at sustaining effort over time

what actually helped me recognize it was talking to someone who had been through it properly, they described the feeling of going through the motions and i realized that was exactly what i'd been doing, showing up, doing the work, meeting the expectations, but not actually being present for any of it

the part that was hardest to sit with is that from the outside nothing looked wrong, deliverables on time, meetings attended, responses sent, burnout is quiet, it doesn't announce itself, it just slowly makes everything feel like it's happening slightly behind glass

i'm not fully out of it, but understanding what it is made it less scary, it's not a character flaw, it's what happens when output stays high for too long without anything coming back in, learning to put things back in is something i'm actively working on now
