two years of writing here

28 December 2025

this blog started in 2024 as a way to think out loud, i didn't expect to still be doing it

what i've noticed is that the writing has changed more than the topics, i'm less interested in explaining things and more interested in just saying what's true, i think that's a better direction even if it's harder to do

2025 was the year of slow and consistent, got the aws cert, went to japan, kept coding in the mornings, didn't ship anything big but built a lot of foundations that i think will matter

next year i want to build something real and put it in the world, not just the site, something that other people can actually use, i'm not sure what yet but the shape of it is starting to come into focus

also the AI stuff is getting genuinely interesting and i want to understand it properly, not just use the tools but understand what's actually happening, i think that's going to be important

see you next year
